Years Of

At ATFAA – Ambika Thamotheram Fine Arts Academy, we are dedicated to the rich heritage of South Indian and Sri Lankan classical fine arts. Founded by Smt. Ambika Thamotheram, a visionary artist and educator, ATFAA serves as a beacon for aspiring artists, nurturing talent and preserving the beauty of traditional art forms.

Smt. Ambika Thamotheram
Leading by Example

Smt. Ambika Thamotheram is an esteemed exponent of South Indian and Sri Lankan classical fine arts. With a deep-rooted passion for art and education, she is the Director of OEBL (Oriental Examination Board London), a prestigious examination body that upholds the standards of classical fine arts education in the UK.

Her commitment to artistic excellence and cultural preservation has made her a respected figure in the fine arts community, inspiring students and artists across generations.

Our

Academy

At ATFAA, we offer expert training in various classical fine arts disciplines, fostering creativity, discipline, and a deep appreciation for cultural heritage. Our academy provides a nurturing environment for students to explore their artistic potential under the guidance of experienced mentors.

02.OEBL – Oriental Examination Board London

As the Director of OEBL, Smt. Ambika Thamotheram plays a crucial role in shaping the future of classical fine arts education in the UK. OEBL is a leading examination body dedicated to assessing and certifying students in various disciplines of South Indian and Sri Lankan fine arts, ensuring excellence and authenticity in training and performance.

Why Choose ATFAA?

Experienced & Passionate Mentorship – Led by Smt. Ambika Thamotheram and a team of expert instructors.

Cultural Authenticity – Learn the true essence of classical fine arts with deep-rooted traditions.

Comprehensive Curriculum – Courses covering both practical and theoretical aspects.

Internationally Recognized Certification – Prepare for OEBL-certified examinations.

A Community of Artists – Connect with fellow students and performers in a vibrant artistic environment.
